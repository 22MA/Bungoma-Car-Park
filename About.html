PARKING SLOTS

Parking of vehicles in Bunoma county has recently used the option of labelling the parking lounge about who and where one can park his/her car.

This sytem provides a more technological other than manual type of allocating where one can park their car.

The system utilizes the following for effectiveness.

  • Computerized allocation of parking slots
  • CCTV controls for monitoring
  • Data collection and storage
  • Human Resource for controling the sytem
  • Computerised parking payment
